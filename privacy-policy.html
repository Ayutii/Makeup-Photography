Privacy Policy

At Ayuti, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the Ethiopian Personal Data Protection Proclamation No. 1321/2024.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect essential information required to provide our services, including:

  1. Name
  2. Email address
  3. Phone number

This information is necessary for purposes such as booking appointments, sending reminders, issuing receipts, and other related activities.

2. Use of Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with third-party platforms such as Telegram, WhatsApp, and Calendly to enhance your experience. Please note that when you are redirected to these services, their respective privacy policies apply. We encourage you to review these policies for more information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  1. Scheduling and managing appointments
  2. Sending service reminders and updates
  3. Issuing receipts and maintaining records

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information. Our systems and processes comply with applicable legal and regulatory standards to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
