Refund Policy

At Ayuti, we strive to provide exceptional services to our clients. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be provided for our services.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

  1. Refunds are only provided for cancellations made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled appointment or service.
  2. For package services, refunds will be calculated based on the services already rendered.
  3. Refunds are not applicable for no-shows or late cancellations.

2. Refund Process

  1. To request a refund, please contact us through email or phone, providing details of your booking and reason for cancellation.
  2. Once approved, refunds will be processed within 7 business days and credited back to the original payment method.
  3. We will notify you once the refund has been initiated.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are not available in the following circumstances:

  • No-shows or failure to attend scheduled appointments.
  • Services already rendered or partially completed.
  • Failure to provide accurate booking information leading to service issues.

4. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding our Refund Policy, please contact us.
